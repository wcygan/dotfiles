# Pre-Trip Preparation Checklist

A personalized countdown from weeks-out to departure day. Adapt based on destination, duration, and travel style.

## 8 Weeks Before Departure

### Documents
- [ ] Passport valid for 6+ months beyond return date
- [ ] Visa requirements researched and applications started (if needed)
- [ ] Travel insurance purchased
- [ ] Digital copies of all documents stored in cloud (passport, visa, insurance, prescriptions)

### Health
- [ ] Vaccination requirements checked (CDC/WHO for destination)
- [ ] Travel medicine appointment scheduled (for exotic destinations)
- [ ] Dental checkup done (don't discover a cavity abroad)
- [ ] Prescriptions refilled with enough supply for trip + 7 extra days

### Planning
- [ ] Accommodation for first and last nights booked
- [ ] Major activities requiring advance booking reserved (permits, popular tours, timed-entry attractions)
- [ ] General itinerary skeleton drafted (not over-planned)
- [ ] Budget envelope set (total and daily target)

---

## 4 Weeks Before Departure

### Logistics
- [ ] Accommodation for remaining nights booked (or intentionally left flexible)
- [ ] Key restaurant reservations made (for special-occasion meals only)
- [ ] Travel insurance details saved to phone and printed
- [ ] Bank/credit card companies notified of travel dates and destinations
- [ ] SIM card or eSIM plan researched and ordered

### Knowledge
- [ ] 20 essential phrases in local language practiced
- [ ] Cultural norms researched (see cultural-preparation.md)
- [ ] One book, film, or documentary about the destination consumed
- [ ] Weather forecast range checked for packing planning

### Group Travel
- [ ] Budget conversation completed with all travelers
- [ ] Shared expense system agreed upon (Splitwise, rotating, kitty)
- [ ] Each person's top priorities identified and shared
- [ ] Emergency contact exchange completed within the group

---

## 2 Weeks Before Departure

### Packing
- [ ] Packing list drafted based on climate and activities (see packing-wisdom.md)
- [ ] Specialty gear acquired, tested, or confirmed (hiking boots broken in, snorkel gear, etc.)
- [ ] Charger and power adapter confirmed for destination's outlet type
- [ ] Day bag / packable backpack ready

### Digital Preparation
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded for destination
- [ ] Translation app with offline pack installed
- [ ] Key apps downloaded (transit, ride-hailing, food delivery for the destination)
- [ ] Boarding passes / e-tickets saved to phone AND email
- [ ] Emergency numbers saved in phone contacts

### Home Preparation
- [ ] Mail hold or neighbor pickup arranged
- [ ] Pet care confirmed
- [ ] Plant watering arranged
- [ ] Thermostat set (energy saving / freeze prevention depending on season)
- [ ] Perishable food used up or given away

---

## 1 Week Before Departure

### Work
- [ ] Out-of-office message drafted
- [ ] Delegate briefed on anything urgent
- [ ] In-flight projects documented for handoff
- [ ] Calendar cleared for return day + 1 (buffer day)

### Final Logistics
- [ ] Airport/station transport planned (not the morning of)
- [ ] Check-in completed online (if available)
- [ ] Carry-on essentials packed separately:
  - [ ] Passport, boarding pass, phone, charger
  - [ ] One change of clothes
  - [ ] Medications (always carry-on, never checked)
  - [ ] Snacks and water bottle for transit
  - [ ] Entertainment for journey
- [ ] Weather forecast checked for departure day and first 3 days

---

## 2 Days Before Departure

### Final Check
- [ ] Everything packed (use the "carry it for 15 minutes" test)
- [ ] Room for souvenirs/return items in luggage
- [ ] Wallet: local currency? Travel card? Backup card stored separately?
- [ ] Phone: charged, data plan activated, key info accessible offline
- [ ] Home: clean enough that coming back isn't depressing

### Mental Preparation
- [ ] Itinerary shared with someone who isn't traveling
- [ ] First night plan clear: how to get from arrival point to accommodation, where to eat
- [ ] Expectations set: the first day is for arriving, not for sightseeing
- [ ] Permission given to yourself: this trip doesn't need to be perfect to be great

---

## Departure Day

### Before Leaving Home
- [ ] Final walk-through: windows closed, water off (long trips), appliances unplugged, lights off
- [ ] Keys to house sitter / neighbor if applicable
- [ ] Triple check: passport, phone, wallet, tickets
- [ ] Photo of your parked car / home exterior (you'll forget where you parked / want peace of mind)

### At the Airport/Station
- [ ] Arrive with time to spare (not rushing sets the trip tone)
- [ ] Phone on airplane mode when boarding (switch to destination SIM/eSIM on arrival)
- [ ] Set watch/phone to destination time
- [ ] Hydrate. Eat something reasonable. The trip has begun

---

## Returning Home

### The Buffer Day
- [ ] Do NOT schedule work or social obligations for the day after return
- [ ] Unpack completely (don't let the suitcase sit for a week)
- [ ] Laundry started
- [ ] Groceries for one day of meals (or order delivery)
- [ ] One thing that makes home feel good: fresh sheets, favorite meal, a walk in your neighborhood

### Within the First Week
- [ ] Photos backed up and organized (before they become an overwhelming backlog)
- [ ] Trip journal/notes finalized (memories fade fast)
- [ ] Thank-you messages sent (hosts, guides, people who helped)
- [ ] Travel insurance follow-up (if any claims needed)
- [ ] Share 3-5 highlights with someone (not a 200-photo slideshow)

See [reentry-integration](reentry-integration.md) for deeper guidance on coming home well.
